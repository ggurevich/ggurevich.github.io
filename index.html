About

Gabriel developed an interest in computers at a young age. After building his first computer in high school, he built, upgraded, and maintained pcs for friends and family. He eventually picked up some networking skills and began installing and maintaining internet connections for others as well. Now he is transitioning into coding to expand his skillset.

As a hardware enthusiast, Gabriel can diagnose equipment issues and offer solutions to repair or replace them. He is also capable of diagnosing network connection issues and resolving them. As a coder Gabriel is excelling in java while obtaining some additional skills in html, css, and javascript.
